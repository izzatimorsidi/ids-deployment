How to Prepare Your Network Traffic Data for Analysis

  1. Open Wireshark and start capturing network traffic on your desired interface.
  2. After capturing the necessary traffic, stop the capture and go to 'File' > 'Export Packet Dissections' > 'As CSV'.
  3. Save the .csv file to your local machine.
  4. Use the form above to upload your .csv file for analysis.

Please ensure that the .csv file contains the necessary traffic data for accurate IDS prediction.
